Low In The Grave He Lay

Verse 1

Low in the grave He lay,
Jesus my Savior!
Waiting the coming day,
Jesus, my Lord:

Chorus:
Up from the grave He arose,
With a mighty triumph o'er His foes;
He arose a Victor

From the dark domain,
And He lives forever
With His saints to reign:
He arose! He arose!
Hallelujah! Christ arose!
