STANDING ON THE PROMISES OF GOD

Verse 1

Standing on the promises of Christ my King,
Through eternal ages let his praises ring;
Glory in the highest, I will shout and Sing,
Standing on the promises of God

Refrain:
Standing, standing,
Standing on the promises of Christ my Saviour;
Standing, standing,
I'm standing on the promises of God.

Verse 2

Standing on the promises that cannot fail,
When the howling storms of doubt and fear
Assail, by the living Word of God I shall
prevail, standing on the promises of God.

Refrain:
Standing, standing, standing on the
Promises of Christ my Saviour;
Standing, standing,
I'm standing on the promises of God.

Verse 3

Standing on the promises of Christ the
Lord, bound to him eternally by love's
strong Cord, Overcoming daily with the Spirit's sword,
Standing on the promises of God.

Refrain:
Standing, standing,
Standing on the promises of Christ my
Saviour; Standing, standing,
I'm standing on the promises of God.

Verse 4

Standing on the promises I cannot fall,
Listening every moment to the Spirit's Call,
Resting in my Saviour as my all in all,
Standing on the promises of God.

Refrain:
Standing, standing,
Standing on the promises of Christ my
Saviour; Standing, standing,
I'm standing on the promises of God.
